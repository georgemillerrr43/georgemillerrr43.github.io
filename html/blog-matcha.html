Matcha And Sourdough

Scroll to read

Sometimes I sit quietly, keeping to myself, usually at a coffee shop. I go there when I need to step away from everything, when I just want some peace and time alone. I usually order a matcha and a piece of sourdough. Then I stare at my matcha, which tastes different than usual and is way too sweet, and at the sourdough that's already down to crumbs. That's when I realize my life feels kind of the same. There's bitterness, there's sweetness, and most of the time it's a mess. But I still enjoy it, because this is my own version of life.

You can’t deny it though, life doesn’t always go smoothly the way we expect. Sometimes I catch myself thinking, “Why does the world feel so unfair?” But then I realize the world doesn’t revolve around me either. Who am I, really? I’m nobody special.

But yeah, whatever. At least I’m not alone. I’m lucky to have people who stay with me and support me, my family, and someone special too. Let’s just call her Olaa.

So yeah, I’m learning to be grateful. I know there are a lot of people out there who have it much harder than I do. But I know that I can’t change that, and I’m thankful for everything that I have.
